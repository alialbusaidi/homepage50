Numbers Speak Louder Than Words

So, you really thought, huh? Well, let me tell you, numbers are everywhere!

From the Fibonacci sequence in nature to the golden ratio in art, numbers shape our world in fascinating ways.

Fun Number Facts

  • The number 42 is the answer to life, the universe, and everything (according to "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy").
  • Zero is the only number that can't be represented in Roman numerals.
  • The number 4 is considered unlucky in many East Asian cultures because it sounds similar to the word for "death".
